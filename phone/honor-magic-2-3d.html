Expert verdict

The Honor Magic 2 3D is suitable for tech-savvy users seeking a high-performance device with advanced camera capabilities and rapid charging. It's ideal for those who value a large, high-quality display and a robust processor. Gamers and photography enthusiasts will appreciate its features.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.39-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Powerful HiSilicon Kirin 980 processor
  • Rapid 40W fast charging
  • High-quality triple camera setup
  • Ample storage options

👎 Cons

  • No major software updates guaranteed
  • No headphone jack
  • No water or dust resistance certification
