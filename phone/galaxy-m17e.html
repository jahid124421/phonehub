Expert verdict

The Galaxy M17e is suitable for casual users who prioritize battery life and camera performance, making it an excellent choice for social media enthusiasts and content creators. Its large display and ample storage also cater to those who enjoy streaming and gaming on the go. However, power users may find the processing capabilities somewhat limited.

👍 Pros

  • Long-lasting 6000 mAh battery
  • High-quality 50 MP primary camera
  • Large 6.7-inch display with a high screen-to-body ratio
  • Ample 128GB storage option

👎 Cons

  • Limited processing power with Mediatek Dimensity 6300
  • No advanced camera features beyond PDAF
  • RAM options could be higher for smoother performance
