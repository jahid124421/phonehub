Expert verdict

The realme P4x 4G is suitable for casual users who prioritize long battery life and a large display, making it ideal for everyday tasks and media consumption. Its camera capabilities also make it a decent choice for photography enthusiasts on a budget. However, it may not be the best fit for heavy gamers or those requiring extensive storage.
