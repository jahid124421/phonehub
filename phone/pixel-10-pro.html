Expert verdict

The Pixel 10 Pro is ideal for photography enthusiasts and power users who value a seamless Android experience. With its high-quality camera system and robust processor, it's perfect for those who want a device that can keep up with their active lifestyle. Additionally, the generous storage options make it suitable for individuals who need ample space for their files and apps.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality camera system with 5x optical zoom
  • Powerful Google Tensor G5 processor
  • Generous storage options up to 1TB
  • Long-lasting 4870 mAh battery
  • Sleek design with 87.6% screen-to-body ratio

👎 Cons

  • Limited RAM options, only 16GB available
  • No headphone jack or microSD card slot
  • Battery capacity could be higher for heavy users
