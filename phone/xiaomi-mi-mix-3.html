Expert verdict

The Xiaomi Mi Mix 3 is suitable for users seeking a high-performance device with ample storage and RAM options, ideal for gaming and multitasking. It also caters to photography enthusiasts with its dual-camera setup. Additionally, its large display and fast charging capabilities make it a great choice for those who consume a lot of media on their phone.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.39-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Powerful Snapdragon 845 processor
  • Dual-camera setup with 4-axis OIS and Dual Pixel PDAF
  • Fast battery charging and wireless charging support
  • Generous RAM and storage options

👎 Cons

  • No headphone jack
  • No microSD card slot for expansion
  • Battery charging speed could be faster
