Expert verdict

The Galaxy A57 is suitable for users seeking a balance between performance and affordability, particularly those who value a high-quality camera and long battery life. It's an excellent choice for everyday use, including social media, photography, and general productivity. However, power users may find the processor to be slightly underwhelming.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality primary camera with OIS
  • Long-lasting 5000 mAh battery
  • Large 6.7-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Multiple storage and RAM options

👎 Cons

  • Processor may not be suitable for heavy gaming or resource-intensive tasks
  • No significant upgrades in design or display technology
