Expert verdict

The Vivo V11 is suitable for users seeking a balance between performance and affordability, particularly those who value a high screen-to-body ratio and fast charging capabilities. It's ideal for everyday use, including social media, photography, and casual gaming. However, power users may find the processor and storage options limiting.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.41-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Fast 18W battery charging
  • Dual camera setup with depth sensor

👎 Cons

  • Processor may not handle demanding tasks efficiently
  • Limited storage options for power users
