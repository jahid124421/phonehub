Expert verdict

The Sony Xperia XZ3 is suitable for photography enthusiasts and individuals seeking a high-performance device with a large display. Its advanced camera features and fast charging capabilities make it an attractive option for those who value convenience and quality. Additionally, the phone's sleek design and robust processor cater to users who demand a premium smartphone experience.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality 19 MP camera with predictive PDAF and laser AF
  • Large 6.0-inch display with 80.5% screen-to-body ratio
  • Fast battery charging with 18W USB Power Delivery 2.0
  • Qi wireless charging support
  • Powerful Qualcomm SDM845 Snapdragon 845 processor

👎 Cons

  • Limited storage capacity at 64 GB
  • No headphone jack
  • No major design innovations compared to predecessors
