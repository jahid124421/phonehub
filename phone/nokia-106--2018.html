Expert verdict

The Nokia 106 is ideal for those seeking a basic, no-frills phone for making calls and sending texts, perfect for elderly individuals or as a backup device. Its simplicity and long battery life make it a great option for people who want a hassle-free phone experience. It's also a good choice for children or individuals who want to minimize their screen time.

👍 Pros

  • Long battery life
  • Simple and easy to use
  • Affordable
  • Durable design

👎 Cons

  • Limited storage capacity
  • Small display
  • No internet capabilities
