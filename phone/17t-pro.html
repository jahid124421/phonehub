Expert verdict

The Xiaomi 17T Pro is suitable for photography enthusiasts and power users who require a large display and long battery life. Its high-quality camera system and fast processor make it an excellent choice for those who want a premium smartphone experience. Additionally, its large storage options cater to users who need ample space for their files and apps.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality camera system with 5x optical zoom
  • Large 6.83-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Long-lasting 7000 mAh battery
  • Fast Mediatek Dimensity 9500 processor
  • Ample storage options up to 1TB

👎 Cons

  • No headphone jack
  • Large battery may contribute to overall weight and size
  • No official IP rating for dust and water resistance
