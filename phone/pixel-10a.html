Expert verdict

The Pixel 10a is ideal for photography enthusiasts and individuals seeking a seamless Android experience, thanks to its high-quality camera and Google's proprietary Tensor G4 processor. This phone is also suitable for everyday users who value a large display and long battery life. Its modest storage options make it less suitable for heavy gamers or those with extensive media libraries.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality primary camera with OIS
  • Large 6.3-inch display with a high screen-to-body ratio
  • Long-lasting 5100 mAh battery
  • Fast Google Tensor G4 processor

👎 Cons

  • Limited storage options with no microSD card slot
  • No major design or display upgrades compared to predecessors
