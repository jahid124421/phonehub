Expert verdict

The Nokia 8 Sirocco is ideal for photography enthusiasts and those seeking a premium Android experience with a focus on durability and sleek design. Its high-quality camera and robust build make it suitable for everyday use. Additionally, it's a good fit for users who value wireless charging and fast charging capabilities.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality dual camera setup
  • Premium design and build
  • Fast charging and wireless charging support
  • Large storage capacity

👎 Cons

  • Outdated processor compared to newer models
  • No headphone jack
  • Limited storage expansion options
