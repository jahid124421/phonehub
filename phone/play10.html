Expert verdict

The honor Play10 is suitable for heavy users who require a large display and long battery life, as well as photographers who want a high-quality primary camera. Its large storage options and decent RAM also make it a good choice for gamers and multitaskers. Overall, it's a solid option for those seeking a balance between performance and affordability.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.8-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Long-lasting 7000 mAh battery
  • High-quality 50 MP primary camera with PDAF
  • Decent storage and RAM options for smooth performance
  • Affordable price point

👎 Cons

  • No detailed specifications on the auxiliary lens
  • No water or dust resistance mentioned
  • Large battery may make the phone bulky
