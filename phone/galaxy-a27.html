Expert verdict

The Galaxy A27 is suitable for everyday users who prioritize a large display and long battery life, making it a great option for those who consume a lot of media on their phone. It's also a good choice for casual photographers who want a reliable camera system. However, it may not be the best fit for heavy gamers or power users who require more advanced features.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.7-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Long-lasting 5000 mAh battery
  • Capable 50 MP primary camera with OIS
  • Variety of storage and RAM options
  • Power-efficient 4 nm processor

👎 Cons

  • Limited macro camera capabilities
  • No advanced features like wireless charging or IP68 rating
  • No high-refresh-rate display option
