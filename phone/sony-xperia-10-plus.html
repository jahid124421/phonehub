Expert verdict

The Sony Xperia 10 Plus is suited for individuals seeking a mid-range smartphone with a decent camera and sufficient storage, ideal for everyday use and casual photography. It caters to those who value a larger display without breaking the bank. This phone is also a good option for users who want fast charging capabilities.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.5-inch display
  • Decent dual-camera setup with 2x optical zoom
  • Fast battery charging with Quick Charge 3.0
  • Sufficient storage options
  • Good screen-to-body ratio

👎 Cons

  • Processor could be more powerful
  • Limited RAM options outside of China
  • No water or dust resistance mentioned
