Expert verdict

The Galaxy A37 is suitable for everyday users who prioritize a large display and long battery life, as well as casual photographers who want a versatile camera setup. It's also a good option for those who want a range of storage and RAM configurations to choose from. Overall, it's a solid mid-range choice.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.7-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Long-lasting 5000 mAh battery
  • Versatile camera setup with wide, ultrawide, and macro lenses
  • Range of storage and RAM configurations available
  • Power-efficient Exynos 1480 processor

👎 Cons

  • No telephoto lens or advanced zoom capabilities
  • No water or dust resistance rating
  • Lower-end models may have limited RAM
