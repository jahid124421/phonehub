Expert verdict

The Turbo 6X Pro is suitable for users seeking a large display and long battery life, making it ideal for multimedia enthusiasts and gamers. Its high-quality camera system also caters to photography hobbyists. Overall, it's a solid choice for those prioritizing performance and entertainment.

👍 Pros

  • Large 6.78-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Long-lasting 8000 mAh battery
  • High-quality 50 MP primary camera with OIS
  • Fast Mediatek Dimensity 7400 Super processor

👎 Cons

  • No higher storage option beyond 256GB
  • No waterproof or dust resistance rating
