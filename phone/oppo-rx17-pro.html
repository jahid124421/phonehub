Expert verdict

The Oppo RX17 Pro is suited for photography enthusiasts and power users who value fast charging and a high-quality camera experience. Its large display and ample storage also make it a great option for those who enjoy multimedia consumption. Overall, it's a solid choice for anyone seeking a balance between performance and camera capabilities.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality camera with Dual Pixel PDAF and OIS
  • Fast charging capabilities with SuperVOOC Flash Charge
  • Large 6.4-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Ample storage options with up to 128 GB and 8 GB RAM

👎 Cons

  • Limited battery life despite fast charging
  • No headphone jack or microSD card slot
  • Processor may not be the latest or most powerful
