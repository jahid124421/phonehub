Expert verdict

The Galaxy S26 is suited for photography enthusiasts and power users who require a fast processor and ample storage. Its large display and long-lasting battery also make it a great option for those who enjoy watching videos or gaming on their phone. Overall, it's a solid choice for anyone seeking a high-end Android experience.

👍 Pros

  • High-quality camera system with optical zoom and image stabilization
  • Fast Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor
  • Large 6.3-inch display with high screen-to-body ratio
  • Long-lasting 4300 mAh battery

👎 Cons

  • No major design innovations
  • No headphone jack
  • Storage options may be too limited for some users
