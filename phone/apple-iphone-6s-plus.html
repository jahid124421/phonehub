Expert verdict

The Apple iPhone 6s Plus is suitable for individuals seeking a high-performance device with an excellent camera and ample storage options, particularly those already invested in the Apple ecosystem. It's ideal for heavy users who require a large display and a robust processor. This phone is also a good fit for those who value ease of use and a premium design.

👍 Pros

  • Large, high-quality display
  • Fast and efficient Apple A9 processor
  • Excellent 12 MP camera with OIS and PDAF
  • Long battery life with up to 384 hours of standby time

👎 Cons

  • Limited RAM at just 2 GB
  • No headphone jack or expandable storage
  • Battery life may vary significantly with heavy usage
