Expert verdict

The Nokia 808 PureView is suitable for photography enthusiasts and those seeking a unique smartphone experience. Its Symbian processor may appeal to users already invested in the ecosystem. However, it may not be the best choice for those seeking a modern, feature-rich operating system.

👍 Pros

  • Impressive camera capabilities
  • Unique design
  • Symbian ecosystem compatibility

👎 Cons

  • Outdated Symbian operating system
  • Limited app selection
  • Processing power may not match modern standards
