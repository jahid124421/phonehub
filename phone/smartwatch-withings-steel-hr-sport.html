Expert verdict

The Withings Steel HR Sport is ideal for fitness enthusiasts and individuals seeking a stylish, hybrid smartwatch. It's suitable for those who want a device that can track their daily activities and receive notifications without the need for a full-fledged smartphone. This watch is perfect for runners, cyclists, and swimmers who need a durable and water-resistant device.

  • Long battery life

  • Heart rate monitoring
  • GPS tracking
