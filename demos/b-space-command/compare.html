COMPARATIVE ASSESSMENT — IPHONE 16 PRO MAX vs GALAXY S25 ULTRA

These two flagship devices represent the pinnacle of their respective ecosystems. The iPhone 16 Pro Max excels with its A18 Pro chip delivering unmatched single-core performance, a refined titanium build that's both lighter and more durable, and Apple's integrated ecosystem. The Galaxy S25 Ultra counters with superior camera hardware (200MP main, 50MP ultrawide), more RAM (12GB vs 8GB), faster charging (45W vs 27W), and the versatile S Pen stylus.

The iPhone wins in build quality (titanium), wireless charging speed, and overall ecosystem integration. The Galaxy S25 Ultra dominates in camera megapixels, RAM capacity, battery size, and wired charging speed. Both share identical IP68 ratings and Wi-Fi 7 support.
