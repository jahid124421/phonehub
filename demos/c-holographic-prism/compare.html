Comparison Summary

Both the iPhone 16 Pro Max and Galaxy S25 Ultra are exceptional flagship devices. The iPhone excels in video capabilities, ecosystem integration, and overall performance optimization. The Galaxy S25 Ultra leads in display brightness, charging speed, camera versatility with its 200MP sensor and dual telephoto lenses, plus the included S Pen.

Choose iPhone 16 Pro Max if: You're invested in the Apple ecosystem, prioritize video recording, and want the best AI integration via Apple Intelligence.
Choose Galaxy S25 Ultra if: You prefer Android, want the best display brightness, need S Pen functionality, and value faster charging with versatile camera zoom options.
